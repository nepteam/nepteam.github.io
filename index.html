Nameless Education Platform

Open-source Development Platform for Collaborative Educational Software

Follow me onGitHub

Why do we make this?

  • improve the quality of the education in developing countries (begin with Thailand)
  • provide a basic platform for developing educational software:
    • the platform must be able to be deployed on a inexpensive low-powered hardware or legacy system.
    • the deployment of the platform must not pose the high maintenance cost in the long term.
    • deployment, maintenance, development and user experience must be simple for users or administrators without experience in advanced computing and networking.
    • the whole ecosystem must be able to operate in any extreme conditions, such as, poor-QOS internet connection, interference, network instability and limited or unstable infrastructure (deficient power supply, extreme heat or humidity).
  • improve students in thought expression, productivity, creativity and collaboration

Projects

The platform is divided into multiple projects.

Foundation

Foundation is the core module providing the security, the basic messaging layer and the basic data management.

Automation

The automation project is to provide the automatic environment setup with Puppet.
